The Family Man

The Family Man is an Indian Hindi-language spy thriller drama streaming
television series created by Raj Nidimoru and Krishna D.K.for the
streaming service Amazon Prime Video and features Manoj Bajpayee
as Srikant Tiwari, a middle-class man secretly working as an
intelligence officer for the Threat Analysis and Surveillance
Cell (TASC), a fictitious branch of the National Investigation Agency.
